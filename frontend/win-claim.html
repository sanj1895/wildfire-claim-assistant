✅ How to Get Your Claim Approved

📜 Required Documents

  • ✅ **Photos of damage (before & after)**
  • ✅ **Receipts for lost or damaged items**
  • ✅ **Copy of your insurance policy**
  • ✅ **Proof of residence (utility bill, lease, mortgage)**

⚠️ Common Claim Denial Reasons

  • ❌ Claim missing required documents
  • ❌ Claim filed too late
  • ❌ Policy doesn’t cover fire damage

🔄 How to Appeal a Denied Claim

  1. 📞 Call your insurance provider for **denial details**.
  2. 📑 Gather missing documents and **write an appeal letter**.
  3. ⚖️ Contact legal aid if your appeal is ignored.
