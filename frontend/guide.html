How to File a Wildfire Insurance Claim

Follow these steps to ensure your claim is processed smoothly.

  1. Ensure Safety First: Do not enter unsafe areas. Take photos from a safe distance.
  2. Contact Your Insurance Company: Call your insurer and report the damage immediately.
  3. Gather Documents: Collect your policy number, photos of damage, receipts, and any related documents.
  4. File Your Claim: Submit your claim online, through an app, or via a call with your insurer.
  5. Meet the Adjuster: An insurance adjuster will inspect the damage. Be prepared to show all documents.
  6. Follow Up: Keep records of all conversations and track your claim status regularly.
